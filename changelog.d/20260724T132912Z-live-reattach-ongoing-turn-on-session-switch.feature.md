Live-reattach an in-progress assistant turn when switching between chat sessions. The foreground `/chat` turn is now mirrored onto the `/events` channel (`chat_turn_started` / `chat_token` / `chat_turn_done`), and the EventBus buffers the current turn per session so a view that connects mid-turn replays what has streamed so far (`chat_turn_resume`) and then follows the live tokens. Switching to a session that is mid-turn now shows the ongoing response streaming (instead of a blank/idle composer that lost the turn), and queued messages stay queued behind it and dispatch only once it completes — fixing the "queued vs. ongoing message" tangle on session switch. A second browser tab on the same session also renders the turn live.
